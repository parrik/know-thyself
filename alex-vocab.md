# Alex — vocabulary

Short glossary of editorial frames, graph-schema terms, and Alex-specific tags
used across the nodes. The dashboard pulls this into the LEGEND panel as a
glossary and into the Today view as pickable definitions.

- **kill your darlings** — Quiller-Couch's editorial maxim (via Perkins): cut a beautiful sentence that does not serve the whole.
- **iceberg theory** — Hemingway's omission principle. Seven-eighths unseen.
- **close reading** — slow, line-by-line attention to what the text actually does, not what it means.
- **acquisitions list** — the set of books an editor is signing this year; a shape, not a quota.
- **in one sitting** — read the manuscript from first page to last without stopping. The whole has a shape a partial read cannot see. (PR02)
- **cut the forest** — monthly discipline: kill commitments that no longer serve the spine. PR03 applied to life, not prose.
- **P03** — the interpretive-depth + emotional-distance pattern. Close to the text, far from the self.
- **N01** — the Helen-structural-presence pattern. Grief structurally present in Alex's editorial posture without being named.
- **risk corridor** — the low-probability, high-consequence cascade that radiates out from O01 (first three months). Each branch has a named canary.
- **canary** — a small, named early-warning signal. When a canary trips, the downstream cascade becomes live, not hypothetical.
- **spine** — the load-bearing observations everything else rests on. If the spine breaks, the graph breaks.
- **overlap** — a pattern grounded in two or more independent observations.
- **novel** — a tentative interpretation. Single derivation, held lightly. Promoted to Overlap only when a second independent instance shows.
- **emergent** — a claim that precipitates only at the intersection of parent nodes. High value, high fragility.
- **forecast** — a time-horizon emergent (1-month, 90-day, 12-month, 5-year). Distinct visual treatment; revisit at horizon, not before.
- **handling** — a directive for how a node wants to be treated (hold lightly, do not propagate, surface only when).
- **political budget** — finite capacity per quarter for public-facing conflict at the press. P02 makes it a variable, not a mood.
- **one corroborating case** — the threshold for promoting Novel → Overlap. One independent instance, not a second reading of the same one.
- **Door County retreat** — PR06, the annual three-day solo retreat. Structural, not therapeutic.
- **the Chen book** — O05, the acquisition that anchors the Chicago list.
